Lake Water

Internship Duration : 3 Months

The Problem statement is : The objective of this project is to find the presence and location of water in a lake using a drone. This can be a challenging task because water bodies can have varying depths, and their surfaces can be covered with vegetation, which makes it difficult to identify the presence of water visually. Additionally, large lakes may require extensive scanning, which can be time-consuming and expensive. A drone equipped with advanced sensors and imaging technology could provide an efficient and cost-effective solution to this problem. The challenge is to develop an accurate and reliable method of detecting and mapping water bodies using a drone.


The Probable Solution is : To precisely identify and plan water bodies in a lake utilizing a robot, a blend of cutting edge sensors and imaging innovation can be used. A multispectral camera that can capture images at various wavelengths of light can be attached to the drone, making it possible to identify water bodies and vegetation. A LiDAR sensor, which can accurately measure the water's depth, can also be added to the drone. A map of the lake, including the locations of water bodies and their depths, would be created from the data gathered by these sensors through computer vision and machine learning methods. Additionally, the drone can be programmed to fly in a predetermined pattern over the lake in order to quickly capture images and data. The arrangement would give an exact and dependable technique for distinguishing and planning water bodies in a lake utilizing a robot, making the undertaking more productive and savvy than customary strategies.
